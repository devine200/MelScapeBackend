ABOUT US

Prepare to have the best rental experience

Get Started

Originally focused on the traditional, low-yield long term rental market in Birmingham, we pivoted to the short term rental market and were blown away by the booking volume, yields and income.

In 2019 the Melscape concept was born to simplify hosting for others with our full-service property management company powered by proprietary human technology. Travellers’ habits are changing, driving demand for homestyle travel accommodation, so Melscape is on a mission to reshape the short term rental market by providing reliable airbnb property management UK wide for property owners, developers and investors.

How we can help

Want to increase your annual returns? Let Melscape show you the revenue potential of short term lets.

Need expertise on managing your property on platforms like Airbnb? The Melscape team are on hand.

Don’t have time for property maintenance and guest relations? Choose Melscape to take care of the nitty-gritty details of hosting.

Struggling to get your property in front of the right guests? Use Melscape professional airbnb hosting services to reach new audiences and vet guests.
